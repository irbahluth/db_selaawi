About The Dataset
The dataset used consists of 7984 rows of data. The processed data is numeric and includes 7 features and 1 label . The dataset consists of 2 classes, namely "Cocok" and "Tidak". The data source comes from a sensor placed in Selaawi, Garut Regency, right in front of the BPP Selaawi office. The focus of this research is on shallot plants. The time span of data collection involves August, September, October, and November 2023, starting from the planting phase, generative phase, vegetative phase, to harvest phase.
